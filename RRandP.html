Routers, Redundancy, and Packets
Routers:
Routers are extremely inportant devices that manage and direct data on networks, they ensure that information reaches it's intended destination efficiently.


Redundancy:
Redundancy ensures uninterrupted communication by using multiple network paths or devices, if one router goes down traffic is rerouted through another.


Packets:
Packets are small units of data that are transmitted across networks in computers. They are important to how data is sent and recieved on the internet.
